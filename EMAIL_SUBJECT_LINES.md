## Tips

1. **Keep it short** - Under 50 characters for best mobile display
2. **Use names** - Personalization increases open rates
3. **Avoid spam words** - Words like "FREE", "URGENT" can trigger spam filters
4. **Test it** - Send test emails to see how it looks in different clients
5. **Be consistent** - Use the same subject format for all invitations

---
